About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Hawaii at Manoa, studying Mathematics and Economics. I previously attended Denison University as a QuestBridge Scholar. For work, I am interested in Data Analytics and work involving Economics. My Data Analytics related interests are Machine Learning, Causal Inference, Systems Dynamics, and Agent-Based Modeling (ABM). In terms of Economics, I am interested in Finance and Industrial Organization.

In my work, I hope to bring underutilized tools to organizations in order to expand their analytical arsenal. In particular, there is extensive work on Systems Dynamics and Agent-Based Modeling, but there is little use of them in industry-applications. Even Causal Inference is something not used extensively, even though many data analytics questions wound up requiring Causal Inference.

In my free time, I like keeping up with the NBA, reading manga, manhwa, and webnovels, as well as learning a new thing here and there. My favorite board game (as you may guess) is Connect Four.
