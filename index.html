My Background / About Me
I'm from Hawaii and am a student at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. I am majoring in mathematics and economics. My core fields of interest are finance and industrial organization, though, I am open to most subjects. I have done research in policy and academia. Additionally, I have done data analytics in industry. I generally consider myself a data analyst because I use data across my work. Aside from economics, I'm a long-time tutor and learning assistant. Because of this, I have an interest in educational psychology, which has made me an effective learner. It also makes me more than happy to teach/mentor people when the time calls for it.
On the side, I enjoy writing, reading, and watching sports. I am a big enjoyer of Dune and Star Wars, and would like to publish a science fiction book one day. Currently, I am enjoying seeing Arsenal FC bottle every trophy imaginable, and every day I pray that the Atlanta Hawks escape the play-in tournament. I'll also occasionally program or make something, which will likely live in my GitHub repo along with this site.
The site still needs some work, as I'm moving some things around from other sites. Regardless, I hope you enjoy it!
